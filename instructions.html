Welcome! Here's a guide to help you understand the main features of the app. Each section below includes an image and a short explanation of what it does.

1. Home Button

Tap the Home button to return to the main screen of the app, where your most recent practice sessions are shown.

2. Settings Icon

This opens the Settings screen where you can adjust preferences like tempo, theme, and practice mode behavior.

3. Upload Button

Use this to upload your progress or export session logs to your email or file storage.

4. Play/Pause Control

This controls the playback of your current practice session loop. You can use it to pause or resume at any time.

5. Help / Info

Tap this icon anywhere in the app to access quick tips and detailed help related to that screen.
