1. Information We Do Not Collect

FretMastery does not collect, store, or process any personal data. We do not track your activity, location, or any other identifying information.

2. Third-Party Services

While we do not collect user data, the App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat – Used to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat does not share personal user data with us. For more details, please review RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy.

3. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised from our end. However, we rely on third-party services for purchases and crash reporting, which have their own security measures.

4. Children's Privacy

5. Your Choices
